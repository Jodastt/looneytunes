LOONEY TUNES

Looney Tunes was a cartoon television show produced by Warner Bros. running from 1930 to 1969. The name was inspired by a symphonic series called 'Merrie Melodies' The show was well recieved, as it won two academy awards for best short subject, it was nominated ten times for academy awards, and was inducted into the national film registry in the year 2000.
